I'm a freelance designer based in Toronto, Canada. I work with small businesses and agencies on a range of design projects including communication, branding, web and interactive experiences. I value design thinking, curiosity, humility and play.


multidisciplinary designer, both creative and strategic curious, great communicator OCAD University graduate with over 6 years of experience in the advertising & design industry, I've grown into an excellent team player and a love for bringing ideas from concept to reality. I consider myself to be a happy and positive person, passionate about all things design. While being exceptionally organized and having a keen eye for detail, I stay motivated, hard-working and curious. When I'm not designing, I'm enjoying brunch, exploring the city, practicing calligraphy, eating ice-cream, reading non-fictions, and keeping up with social media. You will always find me walking on the sunny side. Wes Anderson, Anna Bond, Ina Garten and Jenna Lyons are some of my biggest inspirations. I absolutely adore elephants, dinosaurs, typography, antiques, and have an unhealthy obsession with purchasing sketchbooks. Her design focus lies in creating memorable experiences for users through outside-the-artboard thinking, meticulous attention to detail, and a positive attitude.
