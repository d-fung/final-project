A creative thinker who finds joy in bringing ideas to life. I'm a multidisciplinary designer who works with small businesses and agencies on a range of design projects including communication, branding, web and interactive experiences. My focus lies in creating memorable experiences, meticulous attention to detail, and a positive attitude. With over 6 years of experience in the advertising & design industry, I've grown to be a great communicator both creatively and strategically through my design. I consider myself to be a happy and positive person, passionate about all things design. While being exceptionally organized and having a keen eye for detail, I stay motivated, hard-working and curious. When I'm not designing, I'm 99% eating. I enjoy brunch, exploring the city, punny jokes, reading design & lifestyle blogs, and keeping up with social media. You will always find me walking on the sunny side of the road.

Services






Brand Development

Building a visual brand that conveys your products or services in a unique, intentional way is an essential part of any successful business. I'll help you develop a unique voice, positioning and identity that highlights your personality and connects with your customers.




Print + Packaging

Are you looking for great print or packaging to compliment your brand? Let's work together design something memorable that will stand out in the market. With experience in retail as well as product development, I know what will make an impact with consumers and how to bring it to life.




Web Design

A brand is much more than just a logo on its own - creating a dynamic, unique website will help bring your story to life. Your website will be beautiful and functional so your customers can get the information they need in a digestible, visual format.
